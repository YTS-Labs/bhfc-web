Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pami Flash Cards App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including your flashcard decks, study progress, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser type for optimization purposes.
  • Local Storage: Your flashcard data is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Pami Flash Cards App service
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Track your learning progress
  • Send you updates and notifications (if enabled)

4. Data Storage

Your flashcard data is primarily stored locally on your device. We do not transfer your personal study data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or backup your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and app improvement. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Export your flashcard data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
